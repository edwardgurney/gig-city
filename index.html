Venue Introduction & blurb

This is the home of music in the midlands. A place where you can come and let your hair down in a safe environment listening to the best DJ’s and bands the UK and the world has to offer.

We don’t have attitudes at Gig City, just a place for like minded people and friends to come and dance together, drink together, listening to the music they love.
